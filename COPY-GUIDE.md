### Newsletter Format
**Subject line formula:** [Specific claim] + [Stakes/benefit]  
Examples:
- "Claude's new plugins saved our legal team 11 hours/week"
- "GPT-5.4 vs Claude Opus: the $47K difference nobody's talking about"

**Structure:**
1. **The headline** (what happened + why it matters to you)
2. **The context** (30 seconds of background)
3. **The analysis** (data, benchmarks, my take)
4. **The bottom line** (what to do about it)
5. **CTA** (reply, share, or read more on beri.net)
