# UX Review — 2026-03-08

## Critical Issues Fixed

### 1. Zscaler Mentions Removed (CRITICAL)
- **`app/newsletter/[slug]/page.tsx`** — Two instances of "Head of AI Engineering at Zscaler" replaced with "AI Engineering Leader" / "AI Engineering Leader · Enterprise AI"
- **`COPY-GUIDE.md`** — Bio reference replaced with "AI engineering leader with experience at Fortune 500 companies"

### 2. Text Contrast Improved (HIGH)
Low-contrast text was widespread. Bumped opacity levels:
- Featured excerpt: `white/45` → `white/60`
- Card excerpts: `white/35` → `white/50`
- Value prop descriptions: `white/40` → `white/55`
- Newsletter signup description: `white/45` → `white/60`
- Footer description: `white/35` → `white/50`
- Related article excerpts: `white/35` → `white/50`

### 3. Subscribe Prompt Overload Reduced (HIGH)
Article pages had **5 subscribe touchpoints** (header button, InlineSubscribe, ScrollSubscribePrompt, ExitIntent, bottom NewsletterSignup). Reduced to **3**:
- Header Subscribe button (persistent, non-intrusive)
- InlineSubscribe (mid-article, contextual)
- Bottom NewsletterSignup (end of article)
- ~~ExitIntent popup~~ — removed
- ~~ScrollSubscribePrompt floating card~~ — removed

### 4. Fabricated Social Proof Removed (HIGH)
- "Trusted by 500+ engineering leaders" → "For engineering leaders" (no inflated subscriber count on a new newsletter)
- Fake testimonial replaced with authentic author bio section
- ExitIntent "500+" claim also removed

### 5. Ambient Effects Toned Down (MEDIUM)
- Background glow orbs reduced: `purple-900/20` → `/12`, `fuchsia-900/15` → `/8`
- Grid pattern opacity: `50` → `30`
- Keeps the dark premium feel without distracting from content

## Issues Noted (Not Fixed — Lower Priority)
- Privacy/Terms links in footer are `href="#"` — need real pages
- No `<meta name="description">` on homepage
- Newsletter form doesn't actually submit to Beehiiv (needs integration)
- Category pills scroll horizontally but no visual affordance indicating more items exist
