## Linking Strategy

### Target: 3-5 internal links per article

**Placement:**
1. **Contextual mentions** - Natural references to related topics
2. **"Related reading" callouts** - Explicit recommendations
3. **Bottom "Continue Reading" section** - 2-3 related articles

**Anchor Text Best Practices:**
- Use descriptive phrases (not "click here")
- Include keywords naturally
- Vary anchor text for same destination
- Keep under 5 words when possible
